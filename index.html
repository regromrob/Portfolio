Introduction

Put a puzzle in front of me and I won't stop until I solve it. For me, Software Engineering is about that "YESS!" feeling you get when you see your work come to life & everything makes sense again. That gratification is unmatched. I am motivated by the power technology have to impact the life of its users.

I'm Regina Robinson, a Full-Stack Software Engineer based in New York City. I have an extensive knowledge in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React & Node.js. My goal is to make meaningful web applications that can bring about positive changes & leave a lasting impact. I'm excited to join a team that allows me to combine my previous experience with prioritizing user/customer experience & my love for problem solving; encourages continuous curiosity for personal growth; and support my passion to create impactful and accessible applications. Take a look at some of my projects.

When I'm not challenging myself on a new project, you can find me dancing around to music with friends, volunteering at a local charity, looking up my next travel destination, or binging old Disney Channel original movies for nostalgic purposes.

Taco Bout' It

JavaScript, CSS, Node.js, Express.js, MongoDB , Mongoose

Taco Bout' It is a CRUD app that allows you to upload and review your favorite taco restaurant. You can scroll on your feed to see reviews by others and like it if it makes you salivate. Change your mind about a review you made? Just delete it. Finding a restaurant for "Taco Tuesday" just got more exciting! Check it out
